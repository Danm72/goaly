# Lead Screening

## Green Flags
- Clear problem description (not buzzword soup)
- Existing product or revenue (not just an idea)
- Realistic timeline and budget expectations
- Found you through content or referral (pre-sold on your approach)
- Willing to discuss budget directly

## Red Flags
- AI buzzword overload without clarity on actual needs
- Vague or stock website (no verifiable product)
- "ASAP" + ambitious scope + no budget mentioned
- Wants free prototype before committing
- Title shifting across platforms (CEO on LinkedIn, Founder on website)
- Expects you to work on spec

## Screening Process
1. Research: LinkedIn, company website, tech signals, Gmail history
2. Assess fit: Does this match your ikigai? (score 0-4)
3. Qualifying questions: Budget, timeline, decision maker, previous tech partners
4. Decision: Engage, pass, or refer
