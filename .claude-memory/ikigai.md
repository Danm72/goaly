# Ikigai Alignment Framework

Use this to filter tasks, projects, and opportunities against what actually matters to you.

## What I Love (Energy Check)
- [What kind of work energizes you?]
- [What do you look forward to doing?]
- [What would you do even if you weren't paid?]

## What I'm Good At (Competence Check)
- [Your top professional skills]
- [What do others come to you for?]
- [Where do you have 10+ years of experience?]

## What the World Needs (Impact Check)
- [What problems do you care about solving?]
- [Where can you create real value?]
- [What would the world miss if you stopped?]

## What I Can Be Paid For (Revenue Check)
- [What services generate revenue?]
- [What products could you build?]
- [Where is the market demand?]

## Scoring Tasks Against Ikigai

| Dimension | Question | Score (0-1) |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| Love | Does this energize me? | |
| Good At | Am I the right person? | |
| Needs | Does this create real value? | |
| Paid For | Does this generate revenue? | |

- **4/4 or 3/4**: Core work — prioritize aggressively
- **2/4**: Supporting work — acceptable, schedule appropriately
- **1/4**: Misaligned — consider delegating or declining
- **0/4**: Red flag — question why you're doing this

## Intersections (Sweet Spots)
- **Passion + Vocation**: [Where love meets skill]
- **Vocation + Mission**: [Where skill meets need]
- **Mission + Profession**: [Where need meets revenue]
- **Profession + Passion**: [Where revenue meets love]
